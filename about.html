About EcoScan

Creating a generation of Sustainably Informed Consumers.

Our Mission

In a world full of choices, making the right one for the planet can be confusing. Misleading labels and complex recycling rules often leave well-intentioned consumers feeling powerless. EcoScan was born from a simple idea: what if everyone could have instant, clear, and actionable information about a product's environmental impact right in their pocket?

Our mission is to demystify sustainability. We empower you to make conscious choices by providing two simple scores—a Waste Score for packaging quantity and a Recyclability Score based on the practical realities of India's recycling infrastructure. We want to turn every shopping trip into an opportunity to make a positive impact.

How It Works

EcoScan operates on a simple "Scan → Score → Act" model. By scanning any product's barcode, you get immediate feedback. But we don't just give you data; we give you a plan. Our actionable suggestions help you reduce waste, find creative ways to reuse packaging, and recycle correctly. Every action's impact is quantified, showing you the tangible difference you're making.

Join Our Community

Our database is built by and for the community. If you scan a product that isn't in our system, you can quickly submit it. This collaborative approach ensures we are always growing and focusing on the products that matter most to you. Together, we can build a comprehensive guide to sustainable consumption in India and drive real change—one scan at a time.
